About

As a full-time, Computer Science student at San Diego State University, I aspire to change the way users, hardware, and software interact with each other. Often, we consider the internet of things to be a "modern inconvenience." For that reason, I use my programming knowledge and social skills to improve all areas of communication in my work.

Outside of school or work, I do things such as: participate in game jams, modify game controllers, modify games, or just play video games. As much as I enjoy video games, I try to get away from the screen by playing music on the piano, drums, ocarina, and guitar.
